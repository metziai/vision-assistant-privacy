Privacy Policy for Vision Assistant

Effective Date: [06.06.2025]

1. Overview

Vision Assistant is designed to help users, including those with visual impairments, describe and understand their surroundings using voice and camera functionality. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data unless explicitly stated.

Images: Photos taken are processed in-memory and may be temporarily sent to trusted AI services to generate descriptions. They are not stored on our servers.

Voice Input: Speech is processed locally and may utilize Apple's built-in speech recognition. We do not store voice data.

Device Information: We do not collect your IP address, location, or device identifiers.

3. Third-Party Services

Vision Assistant interacts with trusted AI service providers solely to generate image descriptions and answer follow-up questions. These services may temporarily receive image or voice data to process your request.

We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Alibaba Cloud Privacy Policy

4. No Tracking or Analytics

Vision Assistant does not use advertising, third-party analytics, cookies, or tracking SDKs.

5. Your Rights

You are not required to create an account or provide any identifiable information to use the app.

If you contact us via email, we will use your email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

6. Compliance

Vision Assistant complies with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13.
