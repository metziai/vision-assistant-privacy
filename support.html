Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Vision Assistant?
A: Vision Assistant is a voice-powered camera app designed to help visually impaired users understand their surroundings through spoken image descriptions.

Q: Do I need a subscription to use the app?
A: You can take a limited number of photos for free. Subscriptions unlock unlimited access.

Q: What languages are supported?
A: Vision Assistant supports English, German, French, Spanish, Chinese, and many more.

Q: Is my data stored or tracked?
A: No. Vision Assistant does not store your photos or speech recordings. For more, see our Privacy Policy.
